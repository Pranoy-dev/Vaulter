PLANNING PERMISSION CERTIFICATE
================================
Local Planning Authority : Wokingham Borough Council
Application Reference    : WBC/2017/1943/FUL
Decision Date            : 8 March 2018
Site Address             : Riverside Business Park, Thames Valley, RG1 8PQ
Applicant                : Riverside Holdings plc

DECISION: PLANNING PERMISSION GRANTED

PROPOSAL
--------
Full application for comprehensive refurbishment and extension of existing
office campus. Works include: new glazed entrance atrium (Unit 1), EPC
upgrading works to all units, installation of roof-mounted solar PV panels
(120 kWp), reconfiguration of car parking to 180 spaces, landscaping works
and installation of EV charging points (20 units).

CONDITIONS
----------
1. The development shall be carried out in accordance with the approved
   plans (Drawing Nos. RBP-2017-100 to RBP-2017-118) unless otherwise
   agreed in writing with the LPA.
2. All external materials shall match existing unless approved in writing.
3. The solar PV installation shall not exceed 120 kWp capacity.
4. EV charging points shall be operational prior to first occupation of
   the refurbished buildings.
5. A Construction Management Plan shall be submitted and approved prior
   to commencement of works.
6. Prior to occupation, an updated EPC assessment (minimum Band B) shall
   be submitted to the LPA.
7. Landscaping scheme as submitted shall be implemented within the first
   available planting season following completion of works.

This permission is granted subject to the Town and Country Planning Act 1990
and the Town and Country Planning (Development Management Procedure)
(England) Order 2015.

CERTIFICATE OF LAWFUL USE
--------------------------
Issued: 12 November 2018
Confirming lawful use of the property as office (Class E(g)(i)) following
completion of refurbishment works.
