EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
The property presents in good overall condition commensurate with its age
(1998 construction, 2018 refurbishment). No significant structural defects
were identified during inspection. The refurbishment appears of adequate
quality. A number of minor maintenance items are noted below (Condition
Rating 2), none of which is considered to be of immediate material concern.

No Condition Rating 3 (urgent) items were identified.

CONDITION RATINGS SUMMARY
--------------------------
Rating 1 (No repair required): Roof (post-refurb membrane), structure,
  foundations, internal fit-out, heating systems, electrical (recently
  upgraded 2021), plumbing, lifts (serviced 2024).

Rating 2 (Repairs/replacement needed but not urgent):
  - External render to Unit 3 north elevation — minor cracking.
    Estimated cost: £8,000–12,000. Recommend within 2 years.
  - Expansion joints on Unit 2 external walkway — slight deterioration.
    Estimated cost: £3,500. Recommend within 12 months.
  - Replacement of 3 roof lights in Unit 1 atrium showing minor sealing
    failure. Estimated cost: £6,000. Recommend within 12 months.

Rating 3 (Urgent): NONE

ESTIMATED MAINTENANCE COSTS (10-YEAR CAPEX)
--------------------------------------------
Year 1–2     : £22,000 (ratings 2 items, plus planned redecoration Unit 4)
Year 3–5     : £45,000 (mechanical/electrical servicing, car park resurfacing)
Year 6–10    : £90,000 (estimated roof membrane replacement contribution,
                         life-cycle replacements)
Total 10-year: £157,000

ENVIRONMENTAL AND REGULATORY MATTERS
--------------------------------------
Asbestos: An Asbestos Management Survey was carried out in 2018 (ref:
  AMS-RBP-2018). No ACM materials identified requiring removal.
  Register held by property manager.
EPC: All units rated Band B (ratings 38–44) — compliant with MEES 2025
  requirements (Band D minimum for commercial lettings).
  Current EPCs valid until 2028.
Fire Safety: Fire Risk Assessment updated October 2025. No outstanding
  actions. Compliant with Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005.
Drainage: CCTV drainage survey conducted October 2023. No blockages or
  structural defects identified.

CONCLUSION
-----------
The property is considered to be in a good state of repair. The identified
Condition Rating 2 items should be addressed within the timescales indicated.
Subject to satisfactory legal and environmental enquiries, the building is
considered suitable for investment purchase.
