DEED OF VARIATION (LEASE AMENDMENT)
=====================================
DATED: 15 September 2023

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD : Riverside Holdings plc ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT   : TechCorp UK Limited ("the Tenant")

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
This Deed of Variation relates to the Lease dated 1 April 2020 between
the Landlord and Tenant in respect of Unit 1, Riverside Business Park,
Thames Valley, RG1 8PQ.

BACKGROUND
----------
The Tenant has requested additional car parking allocation and a licence
to carry out fit-out works to install a server room within Unit 1.
The parties have agreed to vary the Original Lease on the terms below.

AGREED VARIATIONS
-----------------
1. CAR PARKING
   Clause 4.3 of the Original Lease is deleted and replaced with:
   "The Tenant shall have the exclusive use of 65 car parking spaces
   (increased from 55) within the Business Park car park, identified
   on the plan attached at Schedule 1 to this Deed."

2. ADDITIONAL RENT — CAR PARKING SUPPLEMENT
   With effect from 1 October 2023, an additional sum of £12,000 per
   annum (£1,000 per month) is payable in respect of the 10 additional
   spaces. This additional rent is to be reviewed in line with
   Clause 2.2 of the Original Lease.

3. LICENCE TO ALTER — SERVER ROOM
   The Landlord grants a licence to the Tenant to carry out the
   following works to Unit 1:
   - Installation of a raised floor data/server room (approx 400 sq ft)
   - Enhanced cooling system (CRAC units) within the demised area
   - UPS installation within the comms room
   Subject to the Tenant: (a) appointing a Landlord-approved contractor;
   (b) providing method statements and drawings prior to commencement;
   (c) reinstating the area to base specification on lease expiry unless
   the Landlord confirms in writing that reinstatement is not required.

4. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as varied by this Deed, all terms and conditions of the Original
   Lease remain in full force and effect.

ANNUAL RENT (REVISED TOTAL FROM 1 OCTOBER 2023)
-------------------------------------------------
Base rent (post-2025 review): £369,200
Parking supplement          : £  12,000
                              ---------
Total passing rent           : £381,200 per annum
