DEED OF VARIATION — RENT REVIEW AND SERVICE CHARGE
=====================================================
DATED: 28 March 2024

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD: Riverside Holdings plc ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT  : GlobalOps Finance Limited ("the Tenant")

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 March 2019 in respect of Unit 2, Riverside Business Park,
Thames Valley, RG1 8PQ.

BACKGROUND
----------
The rent review due on 1 March 2024 has been agreed between the parties.
In addition, the parties wish to vary the service charge cap mechanism
following the 2023 service charge reconciliation.

VARIATIONS
----------
1. RENT REVIEW — MARCH 2024
   Pursuant to Clause 2.2 of the Lease, the parties agree the upward-only
   open market rent review as follows:

   Previous annual rent   : £231,000 per annum (£22.00 per sq ft)
   Reviewed annual rent   : £262,500 per annum (£25.00 per sq ft)
   Effective from         : 1 March 2024

   Comparable evidence relied upon (summary):
   - Thames Valley Grade A lettings Q4 2023 – Q1 2024 averaging £24.50–£25.50/sq ft
   - Valuer: Thornton Valuation Consultants LLP (acting for Landlord)
   - Tenant's surveyor: C&W Advisory (agreed without referral to arbitration)

2. SERVICE CHARGE CAP
   Clause 4.1 of the Lease is varied to add the following sub-clause:

   "4.1A The Tenant's contribution towards the Service Charge shall not
   increase by more than 5% per annum (compounded) above the Base Year
   amount. The Base Year shall be the service charge year ending 28 February
   2024 (£48,000). This cap shall not apply to the insurance element of the
   Service Charge or to items of exceptional expenditure approved by the
   Landlord acting reasonably."

   Current service charge: £50,400 per annum (£4.80/sq ft)
   Year 1 cap (Mar 2024 – Feb 2025): £50,400 (within cap)

3. SUITE 2A — POST SUB-LEASE RE-OCCUPATION
   The parties record that:
   (a) The sub-lease of Suite 2A (2,100 sq ft) to Nexus Solutions Ltd
       expired on 31 May 2025;
   (b) GlobalOps Finance Ltd re-occupied Suite 2A from 1 June 2025;
   (c) No further sub-letting consent is required for the Tenant's own
       occupation of Suite 2A;
   (d) The passing rent and service charge apply to the entire Unit 2
       (10,500 sq ft) as a single demise.

4. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as varied by this Deed, all terms of the Lease continue in full
   force and effect.

EXECUTION STATUS: FULLY SIGNED AND EXECUTED
