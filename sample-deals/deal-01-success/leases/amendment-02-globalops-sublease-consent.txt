LICENCE TO SUB-LET — SUITE 2A
================================
DATED: 20 May 2022

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD  : Riverside Holdings plc ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT    : GlobalOps Finance Ltd ("the Tenant")
(3) SUB-TENANT: Nexus Solutions Limited ("the Sub-Tenant")
                Company No. 10234567
                Registered Address: 7 Innovation Drive, Bracknell, RG12 1BB

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 March 2019 in respect of Unit 2, Riverside Business Park,
Thames Valley, RG1 8PQ.

BACKGROUND
----------
The Tenant occupies Unit 2 under the Lease. The Tenant has requested the
Landlord's consent to sub-let a portion of the Demised Premises known as
Suite 2A (approximately 2,100 sq ft) on the second floor, shown edged
green on the plan at Schedule 1.

AGREED TERMS
-------------
1. CONSENT TO SUB-LET
   The Landlord consents to the letting of Suite 2A to the Sub-Tenant on
   the following terms:

   Sub-Lease Term     : 3 years from 1 June 2022 to 31 May 2025
   Sub-Lease Rent     : £46,200 per annum (£22.00/sq ft)
   Break Option       : None
   Rent Free          : 2 months (June–July 2022, incentive)
   Use                : Class E(g)(i) — IT consultancy and software services

2. CONDITIONS OF CONSENT
   (a) The Tenant remains primarily liable for all obligations under the
       Lease including payment of the full rent for Unit 2;
   (b) The Sub-Tenant shall not further sub-let or assign without the
       prior written consent of the Landlord and the Tenant;
   (c) The Sub-Tenant shall observe all relevant covenants in the Lease;
   (d) On expiry of the sub-lease, the Tenant shall procure that Suite 2A
       is reinstated to its condition at the date of this Licence (reference
       photographic schedule at Schedule 2);
   (e) The Tenant shall register a notice of the sub-lease at Land Registry
       within 28 days of completion.

3. RENT ALLOCATION
   For clarity, the rent payable by the Tenant to the Landlord under the
   Lease (currently £231,000 per annum) is not reduced by this sub-letting.
   The sub-lease rent of £46,200 per annum is for the Tenant's own account.

4. COSTS
   The Tenant shall pay the Landlord's reasonable legal costs of this
   Licence (£1,800 + VAT agreed).

5. EXPIRY / TERMINATION
   Upon expiry of the Sub-Lease on 31 May 2025, the Landlord's consent
   shall lapse automatically. Any extension or renewal of the sub-letting
   shall require a fresh Licence.

EXECUTION STATUS: FULLY SIGNED AND EXECUTED BY ALL THREE PARTIES

This Licence has been duly executed as a deed by the Landlord, Tenant and
Sub-Tenant. All signatures verified and witnessed. Sub-lease registered at
Land Registry (notice dated 3 June 2022). Original held by Freeths LLP.
Sub-lease expired 31 May 2025 — no further action required.
