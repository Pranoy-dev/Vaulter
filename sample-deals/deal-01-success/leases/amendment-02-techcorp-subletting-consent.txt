LICENCE TO SUB-LET (PART)
==========================
DATED: 12 January 2024

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD  : Riverside Holdings plc ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT    : TechCorp UK Ltd ("the Tenant")
(3) SUB-TENANT: DataStream Analytics Limited ("the Sub-Tenant")
                Company No. 12345678
                Registered Address: 14 Silicon Way, Cambridge, CB1 2FX

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 April 2020 as varied by Deed of Variation dated 15 September
2023 ("the Lease") in respect of Unit 1, Riverside Business Park, Thames
Valley, RG1 8PQ.

RECITALS
--------
A. The Tenant has requested the Landlord's consent to sub-let approximately
   3,200 sq ft of the Demised Premises (the south wing of Unit 1, as shown
   hatched blue on the attached plan at Schedule 2) ("the Sub-Let Area").

B. The Landlord has agreed to grant such consent subject to the conditions
   set out in this Licence.

AGREED TERMS
-------------
1. CONSENT TO SUB-LET
   The Landlord consents to the Tenant sub-letting the Sub-Let Area to the
   Sub-Tenant on the terms of the draft sub-lease initialled by both parties
   and annexed at Schedule 1.

   Sub-Lease Term : 2 years from 1 February 2024 to 31 January 2026
   Sub-Lease Rent : £89,600 per annum (£28.00/sq ft)
   Permitted Use  : Class E(g)(i) — Data analytics and software development
   Break Option   : None

2. CONDITIONS OF CONSENT
   (a) The Tenant shall remain primarily liable for all obligations under
       the Lease notwithstanding the sub-letting;
   (b) The Sub-Tenant shall not further sub-let, assign or part with
       possession of the Sub-Let Area;
   (c) The Tenant shall procure that the Sub-Tenant complies with all
       covenants in the Lease applicable to the Sub-Let Area;
   (d) The Sub-Tenant shall enter into a direct covenant with the Landlord
       to observe the covenants in the head lease;
   (e) On expiry or sooner determination of the sub-lease, the Sub-Let Area
       shall be reinstated to open-plan specification at the Tenant's cost.

3. REGISTRATION
   The Tenant shall provide the Landlord with a certified copy of the
   executed sub-lease within 14 days of completion.

4. COSTS
   The Tenant shall pay the Landlord's reasonable legal costs of granting
   this Licence (capped at £2,500 + VAT).

5. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as expressly provided in this Licence, all terms of the Lease
   remain in full force and effect.
