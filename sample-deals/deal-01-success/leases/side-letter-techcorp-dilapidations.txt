SIDE LETTER — DILAPIDATIONS PROTOCOL
======================================
DATED: 22 November 2025

FROM: Riverside Holdings plc ("Landlord")
TO  : TechCorp UK Ltd ("Tenant")

RE  : Unit 1, Riverside Business Park, Thames Valley, RG1 8PQ
      Lease dated 1 April 2020 (as varied)

Dear Sirs,

I refer to the above Lease and to discussions between our respective
property managers regarding the forthcoming break option on 31 March 2028.

The parties have agreed the following protocol in respect of dilapidations
and terminal schedule of condition, to apply without prejudice to the
Landlord's full rights under the Lease:

1. INTERIM INSPECTION
   The Landlord's surveyor shall have the right to conduct an interim
   dilapidations inspection of Unit 1 no earlier than 12 months before
   the Break Option Date (i.e. from 1 April 2027). The Tenant shall
   provide reasonable access on not less than 10 working days' notice.

2. SCHEDULE OF DILAPIDATIONS
   Should the Tenant elect to exercise the Break Option, the Landlord
   shall serve a terminal schedule of dilapidations no later than
   3 months prior to the Break Option Date. The Tenant shall have
   60 days to agree or dispute items on the schedule.

3. SERVER ROOM REINSTATEMENT
   With reference to the Deed of Variation dated 15 September 2023
   (Clause 3 — Licence to Alter — Server Room), the Landlord confirms
   that, subject to the server room being in good condition and the
   raised floor undamaged, the Landlord SHALL NOT require reinstatement
   of the server room to base specification on break / expiry. The
   enhanced cooling system (CRAC units) and UPS installation shall
   remain in situ.

   This confirmation saves the Tenant an estimated reinstatement cost
   of £145,000–£175,000 (based on QS estimate dated October 2025).

4. SUB-LET AREA (SOUTH WING)
   The sub-lease to DataStream Analytics Limited expires 31 January 2026.
   On expiry of the sub-lease, the open-plan specification reinstatement
   obligation (per Licence to Sub-Let dated 12 January 2024, Clause 2(e))
   shall apply. The Tenant confirms it has budgeted for these works.

5. PHOTOGRAPHIC RECORD
   Both parties agree to commission an independent photographic schedule
   of condition within 30 days of the date of this letter. The cost shall
   be split equally. The record will be admissible in any dilapidations
   negotiation.

This letter does not operate as a deed and does not vary the terms of the
Lease save to the extent expressly stated above. In the event of conflict
between this letter and the Lease, the Lease shall prevail.
