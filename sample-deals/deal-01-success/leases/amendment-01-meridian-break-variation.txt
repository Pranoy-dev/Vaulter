DEED OF VARIATION — BREAK OPTION
==================================
DATED: 14 August 2025

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD: Riverside Holdings plc ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT  : Meridian Consulting LLP ("the Tenant")

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 July 2020 in respect of Unit 3, Riverside Business Park,
Thames Valley, RG1 8PQ ("the Lease").

BACKGROUND
----------
A. Under Clause 1 of the Lease the Tenant has a break option exercisable
   on 30 June 2027 subject to giving not less than 12 months' prior
   written notice ("the Original Break Provision").

B. The Tenant has approached the Landlord requesting modification of the
   break notice period and the addition of a financial incentive for the
   Tenant to remain beyond the break date.

C. The Landlord wishes to secure income continuity and has agreed to the
   variations set out below.

VARIATIONS
----------
1. BREAK NOTICE PERIOD
   Clause 1 (Break Option) of the Lease is varied as follows:

   The words "not less than twelve (12) months" are deleted and replaced
   with "not less than nine (9) months".

   Revised Break Notice Deadline: 30 September 2026 (being 9 months
   before the Break Date of 30 June 2027).

   All other conditions for exercising the break remain as stated in the
   Lease (no arrears, vacant possession, compliance with covenants).

2. INCENTIVE PAYMENT — BREAK FEE
   The Landlord agrees that if the Tenant does NOT exercise the break
   option and confirms in writing by 30 September 2026 that it intends
   to remain for the full term (to 30 June 2030), the Landlord shall:

   (a) Grant a 2-month rent-free period (October–November 2027) as a
       retention incentive — value: £42,467 (based on current rent);
   (b) Contribute up to £25,000 towards the Tenant's office fit-out /
       refurbishment programme (subject to Landlord approval of works).

   Total incentive value: up to £67,467.

3. RENT REVIEW CONFIRMATION
   The parties record that the upward-only rent review on 1 July 2025
   has been agreed at £254,800 per annum (£26.00/sq ft), an increase
   of £29,400 from the previous passing rent of £225,400 (£23.00/sq ft).
   
   Comparable evidence considered:
   - Thames Valley office lettings Q1–Q2 2025 in the range £25.50–£27.00/sq ft
   - Agreed without referral to independent expert

4. COSTS
   Each party shall bear its own legal costs.

5. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as varied by this Deed, all terms of the Lease remain in full
   force and effect.
