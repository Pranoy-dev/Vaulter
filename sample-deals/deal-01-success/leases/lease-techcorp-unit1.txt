1. DEMISE AND TERM
------------------
1.1 The Landlord demises the Property to the Tenant for a term of
    TWELVE (12) YEARS commencing on 1 April 2020 and expiring on
    31 March 2032 ("the Term").

1.2 The Tenant shall have a personal, non-assignable option to determine
    this Lease on 31 March 2028 ("Break Option Date") provided that:
    (a) The Tenant serves not less than twelve (12) months' prior written
        notice on the Landlord;
    (b) All rents and sums due under this Lease are paid up to date;
    (c) Vacant possession is given on the Break Option Date.

2. RENT
-------
2.1 Initial Annual Rent: £340,800 per annum (£24.00 per sq ft)
    Payable quarterly in advance on the usual quarter days.

2.2 Rent Review: The rent shall be reviewed on 1 April 2025 and 1 April 2028
    on an upward-only open market rental basis.

2.3 Post-Review Rent (agreed April 2025): £369,200 per annum (£26.00 per sq ft)

3. RENT ESCALATION
------------------
3.1 Following each rent review, rents shall be escalated in accordance with
    upward-only open market review provisions. Cap: 25% increase per review
    period; Collar: 0%.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
-------------------
4.1 A rent deposit of £92,300 (equivalent to three months' rent) is held
    by the Landlord in a designated deposit account (account ref: RD-TC-001).

5. PERMITTED USE
----------------
5.1 Class E(g)(i) — Offices. The Tenant shall not use the Property for any
    other purpose without the Landlord's prior written consent.

6. SERVICE CHARGE
-----------------
6.1 The Tenant shall contribute to the service charge in the proportion that
    the NIA of the Property bears to the total NIA of the Business Park.
    Current Service Charge: £68,160 per annum (£4.80 per sq ft).
    Budget Year: 1 April 2025 to 31 March 2026.

7. REPAIRING OBLIGATIONS
------------------------
7.1 Full Repairing and Insuring (FRI) lease. The Tenant shall keep the
    Property in good and substantial repair and condition throughout the Term.

8. ALIENATION
-------------
8.1 The Tenant may assign the whole of the Property with the Landlord's
    prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld).
8.2 Sub-letting of the whole or parts is permitted with Landlord consent.

9. ALTERATIONS
--------------
9.1 No structural alterations without Landlord consent.
9.2 Internal, non-structural alterations permitted with prior written consent.

10. GUARANTOR OBLIGATIONS
--------------------------
10.1 The Guarantor guarantees punctual payment of all rents and performance
     of all Tenant obligations throughout the Term.
