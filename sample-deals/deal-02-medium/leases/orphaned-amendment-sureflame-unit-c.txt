PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD: Midlands Property Fund II LP ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT  : SureFlame Heating Distribution Ltd ("the Tenant")

LEASE REFERENCE
---------------
Lease dated 15 March 2019 in respect of Unit C, Central Trade Estate,
Coventry Road, Birmingham, B26 3QR.

BACKGROUND
----------
Under the original Lease, the Tenant operates a heating and plumbing
supplies trade counter from Unit C (7,500 sq ft NIA). The Tenant has
requested consent to extend operating hours and install a mezzanine.

NOTE: This amendment relates to the PREVIOUS tenant of Unit C. SureFlame
Heating vacated Unit C on 30 September 2025. The base lease for SureFlame
is not available in the data room — the vendor has confirmed the original
was misplaced during a property management transition in 2023.

VARIATIONS
----------
1. OPERATING HOURS
   Clause 7 (Use Restrictions) of the Lease is varied to:
   "The Tenant may use the Property between the hours of 06:00 and 21:00
   Monday to Saturday and 08:00 to 17:00 on Sundays, provided that the
   Tenant complies at all times with the Planning Permission conditions
   and Environmental Health requirements."

   Previous permitted hours: 07:00–19:00 Monday to Friday, 08:00–14:00
   Saturday. Sunday trading was not previously permitted.

2. MEZZANINE LEVEL
   The Landlord grants consent for the Tenant to install a steel mezzanine
   floor within Unit C:
   - Approximate area: 2,200 sq ft
   - Purpose: Additional storage of heating components
   - Structural engineer's approval: Certificate dated 20 May 2024
   - Building Regulations application submitted: ref B26/2024/0312

   Reinstatement: Required on lease expiry unless Landlord waives in
   writing. Estimated reinstatement cost: £28,000.

3. ADDITIONAL RENT
   With effect from 1 July 2024, additional rent of £6,600 per annum
   (£3.00/sq ft of mezzanine) is payable. Total rent from 1 July 2024:

   Ground floor rent: £64,800 per annum (£8.64/sq ft)
   Mezzanine rent   : £ 6,600 per annum
   Total            : £71,400 per annum

4. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as varied, all terms of the original Lease remain in force.
