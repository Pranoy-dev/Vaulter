DEED OF LICENCE — ALTERATIONS
===============================
DATED: 14 March 2021

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD: Riverside Holdings plc ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT  : Precision Manufacturing Ltd ("the Tenant")

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 October 2018 in respect of Unit A, Central Trade Estate,
Coventry Road, Birmingham, B26 3QR ("the Lease").

BACKGROUND
----------
The Tenant has applied for the Landlord's consent to install a spray-
painting booth and associated extraction system within the Demised
Premises. The Landlord has agreed to grant a licence for the works on
the terms below.

LICENCE
-------
1. PERMITTED WORKS
   The Landlord licenses the Tenant to carry out and complete the
   following works to Unit A ("the Works"):

   (a) Installation of a dry-filter spray-painting booth (approx.
       6m × 4m footprint) within the rear bay area;
   (b) Installation of an external extraction system with ducting
       routed through the rear wall to a roof-mounted discharge unit;
   (c) Installation of compressed air supply serving the booth;
   (d) Associated electrical works (3-phase supply extension, emergency
       isolator, extraction fan motor).

   The Works shall be carried out in accordance with the specifications
   and drawings submitted by the Tenant (reference: PM-SPB-2021-Rev3)
   and approved by the Landlord's building surveyor on 10 February 2021.

2. CONDITIONS
   (a) All Works shall be completed by a contractor approved in writing
       by the Landlord (approved: Midlands Industrial Fit-Out Ltd);
   (b) The Tenant shall obtain all necessary statutory consents including
       Building Regulations approval and any environmental permits;
   (c) The Tenant shall provide evidence of public liability insurance
       (minimum £5,000,000) from the contractor prior to commencement;
   (d) The Works shall not compromise the structural integrity of the
       building or its fire compartmentation;
   (e) The Tenant shall notify the Landlord's property manager within
       7 days of practical completion of the Works.

3. REINSTATEMENT
   On expiry or sooner determination of the Lease, the Tenant shall at
   its own cost remove the spray-painting booth, extraction system and
   all associated plant and reinstate the Demised Premises to its
   condition prior to the Works, UNLESS the Landlord serves written
   notice waiving the reinstatement obligation not less than 6 months
   before lease expiry.

   Estimated reinstatement cost (2021 QS estimate): £35,000–£45,000.

4. INSURANCE
   The Tenant shall notify the Landlord's insurers of the Works and
   any additional premium required shall be payable by the Tenant.
   Confirmed: additional premium of £850 per annum notified and accepted.

5. COSTS
   The Tenant shall pay the Landlord's reasonable surveyor and legal
   costs of this Licence: surveyor £1,200 + legal £800 = £2,000 + VAT.

EXECUTED as a deed.
