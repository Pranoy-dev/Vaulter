DEED OF VARIATION — STORAGE YARD EXTENSION
=============================================
DATED: 1 November 2023

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD: Midlands Property Fund II LP ("the Landlord")
(2) TENANT  : Apex Logistics Solutions Ltd ("the Tenant")

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 April 2021 in respect of Unit B, Central Trade Estate,
Coventry Road, Birmingham, B26 3QR ("the Lease").

BACKGROUND
----------
The Tenant's logistics operations require additional external storage
and HGV manoeuvring space. The Landlord has agreed to extend the demise
to include the yard area immediately to the rear of Unit B (approximately
3,200 sq ft of hard-standing), previously used as overflow parking.

VARIATIONS
----------
1. EXTENSION OF DEMISE
   The Demised Premises under the Lease are extended to include:
   - The hard-standing yard area edged blue on the plan at Schedule 1
     ("the Yard"), being approximately 3,200 sq ft of concrete hard-
     standing to the rear of Unit B

   The Tenant's total demise is now:
   - Unit B internal: 9,200 sq ft NIA
   - Yard area      : 3,200 sq ft (external)

2. ADDITIONAL RENT
   With effect from 1 November 2023, the Tenant shall pay additional
   rent of £9,600 per annum (£3.00 per sq ft of yard area) in respect
   of the Yard. This is payable quarterly in advance alongside the
   principal rent.

   Total rent payable:
   - Internal rent (pre-review)  : £138,000 (£15.00/sq ft)
   - Yard rent                   : £  9,600 (£3.00/sq ft)
   - Total from 1 Nov 2023      : £147,600

   NOTE: Following the April 2024 rent review, the internal rent
   increased to £158,200 (£17.20/sq ft). The yard rent was NOT subject
   to review. Total from 1 Apr 2024: £167,800.

3. YARD USE RESTRICTIONS
   The Yard may be used only for:
   (a) Marshalling and manoeuvring of delivery vehicles;
   (b) Temporary storage of palletised goods (maximum 48 hours);
   (c) Overnight parking of the Tenant's own fleet vehicles (maximum 4 HGVs).

   The Tenant shall not store hazardous materials, waste or derelict
   vehicles in the Yard.

4. MAINTENANCE
   The Tenant shall maintain the Yard hard-standing in good condition
   and shall promptly repair any damage caused by the Tenant's use.

5. REINSTATEMENT
   On lease expiry or break, the Tenant shall vacate the Yard, remove
   all goods and vehicles, and return the Yard in clean and clear
   condition. No structural reinstatement is required.

6. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as varied herein, all terms of the Lease continue unchanged.

EXECUTED as a deed.
