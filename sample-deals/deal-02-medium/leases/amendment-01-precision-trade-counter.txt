DEED OF VARIATION (LEASE AMENDMENT)
=====================================
DATED: 1 March 2023

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD : Midlands Property Fund II LP
(2) TENANT   : Precision Manufacturing Limited

ORIGINAL LEASE: Dated 1 October 2018 — Unit A, Central Trade Estate

AGREED VARIATIONS
-----------------
1. PERMITTED USE EXTENSION
   Clause 5 of the Original Lease is varied to add the following permitted use:
   "Trade counter sales direct to trade and public for goods manufactured
   or distributed from the Property, subject to a maximum of 20% of the
   demised area being used for trade counter purposes."
   This variation takes effect from 1 March 2023.

2. SIGNAGE
   The Landlord consents to the installation of a fascia sign above the
   Unit A roller-shutter door ("Precision Manufacturing — Trade Counter")
   in accordance with drawing PM-SIGN-2023-01. The Tenant shall maintain
   the signage in a clean and tidy condition. On lease expiry the Tenant
   shall remove the signage and make good all fixings.

3. ASSIGNMENT RESTRICTION
   In addition to Clause 8 of the Original Lease, the Landlord confirms
   that consent to assignment to a trade counter / retail operator
   (notwithstanding any restriction on retail use) shall not be
   unreasonably withheld provided the proposed assignee is of acceptable
   financial standing.

4. ALL OTHER TERMS
   Save as varied, all terms of the Original Lease remain in force.

NOTE: Updated passing rent at date of variation:
  Current annual rent under Original Lease: £209,600 (post Oct 2023 review)
  No change to rent under this Deed.
