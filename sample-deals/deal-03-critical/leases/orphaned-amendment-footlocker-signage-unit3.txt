DEED OF VARIATION — SIGNAGE AND USE EXTENSION
================================================
DATED: 15 April 2022

PARTIES
-------
(1) LANDLORD: Parkway Retail Fund Ltd
(2) TENANT  : Footlocker Sports Ltd

PROPERTY
--------
Unit 3, Parkway Retail Centre, Manchester Road, Stockport, SK1 3UA

LEASE REFERENCE
---------------
Renewal Lease dated 1 January 2021 (which itself renewed the Original
Lease dated 1 January 2016 — not available in data room).

VARIATIONS
----------
1. SIGNAGE
   The Landlord consents to the installation of:
   (a) An illuminated fascia sign (12m × 1.5m) above the Unit 3 entrance;
   (b) A free-standing A-board on the centre walkway (subject to planning).
   The Tenant shall maintain all signage in good condition and remove on
   lease expiry, making good any fixings.

2. USE EXTENSION
   Clause 5 of the Lease is varied to allow:
   "Class E(a) — Sale of sports goods, footwear, accessories, AND
   sportswear/athleisure clothing."
   (Previously: sports goods, footwear and accessories only.)

3. PROMOTIONAL EVENTS
   The Tenant is permitted to hold in-store promotional events and
   product launches on not more than 12 occasions per calendar year,
   subject to the Tenant providing the Landlord with 14 days' notice.

4. ALL OTHER TERMS unchanged.

EXECUTED as a deed.
