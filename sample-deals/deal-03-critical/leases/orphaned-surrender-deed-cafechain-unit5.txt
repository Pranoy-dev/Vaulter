PROPERTY

Unit 5, Parkway Retail Centre, Manchester Road, Stockport, SK1 3UA

LEASE REFERENCE
---------------
Lease dated 1 October 2020 ("the Lease") — granted for 5 years expiring
30 September 2025.

NOTE: The Lease document is NOT available in this data room. The Landlord's
previous managing agents (Stockport Property Services Ltd) did not transfer
a copy to the Administrators.

RECITALS
--------
A. The Tenant has been in occupation of the Property since October 2020,
   operating a coffee shop and café under the brand "CafeChain".

B. Since Q4 2024, the Tenant's trading at this location has been severely
   impacted by:
   - Declining footfall at Parkway Retail Centre (down 35% vs 2022);
   - Departure of adjacent tenants (HomeStyle from Unit 4, August 2025
     signalled, and general vacancy);
   - The Tenant's own financial difficulties (net loss of £12,400 for
     FY2024 at this branch).

C. The Tenant has requested early surrender of the Lease (7 months before
   contractual expiry). The Landlord has agreed on the terms below.

SURRENDER TERMS
----------------
1. SURRENDER DATE
   The Lease shall be surrendered and cease to have effect from the close
   of business on 31 March 2025 ("the Surrender Date").

2. SURRENDER PREMIUM
   The Tenant shall pay a surrender premium of £8,500 (approximately
   2 months' rent) to the Landlord on or before the Surrender Date, in
   compensation for the loss of rent for the remainder of the contractual
   term.

3. CONDITION HANDBACK
   The Tenant shall:
   (a) Remove all trade fixtures, fittings, furniture, and refuse;
   (b) Make good any damage to the fabric of the building caused by the
       removal of fixtures;
   (c) Leave the Property in a clean and broom-swept condition;
   (d) Decommission and remove the commercial kitchen extraction system
       and cap all gas and grease extract ducts;
   (e) Hand over keys, alarm codes, and utility meter readings by 5pm
       on the Surrender Date.

4. DILAPIDATIONS
   The Landlord agrees that it shall NOT serve a terminal schedule of
   dilapidations provided the conditions at Clause 3 above are fulfilled.
   This is a full and final settlement of all dilapidation claims.

5. RENT AND SERVICE CHARGE
   (a) Rent shall be paid up to and including the Surrender Date (i.e.
       quarterly instalment for Q1 2025 of £11,700 due 1 January 2025 —
       confirmed paid);
   (b) Service charge apportioned to the Surrender Date — £1,050; 
   (c) No further sums shall be payable by either party after the
       Surrender Date.

6. RENT DEPOSIT
   The Tenant's rent deposit of £11,700 (3 months) shall be returned in
   full within 14 days of the Surrender Date. No set-off against the
   surrender premium — the premium is a separate payment.

7. INSURANCE
   The Landlord shall arrange void insurance for Unit 5 from the Surrender
   Date.

8. RELEASE
   Upon fulfilment of the terms above, both parties release each other
   from all obligations under the Lease (including any guarantee).
