SIDE LETTER — RENT CONCESSION
===============================
DATED: 20 June 2024

FROM: Parkway Retail Fund Ltd ("Landlord")
TO  : FashionZone Limited ("Tenant")

RE  : Unit 1, Parkway Retail Centre, Manchester Road, Stockport, SK1 3UA
      Lease dated 1 October 2016

Dear Sirs,

RENT CONCESSION — TEMPORARY REDUCTION
---------------------------------------
Further to our discussions regarding trading conditions at the Centre and
the recent departure of HomeStyle Ltd from Unit 4, the Landlord has agreed
to grant the following temporary rent concession:

1. CONCESSION PERIOD
   6 months: 1 July 2024 to 31 December 2024

2. REDUCED RENT
   During the Concession Period, the annual rent shall be reduced from
   £364,000 (£26.00/sq ft) to £291,200 (£20.80/sq ft) — a 20% reduction.

   Monthly rent during concession: £24,266.67
   Total concession value: £36,400

3. CONDITIONS
   (a) This concession is personal to FashionZone Limited and not
       assignable to any successor tenant;
   (b) FashionZone shall keep the store open and actively trading for a
       minimum of 5.5 days per week during the Concession Period;
   (c) FashionZone shall not further reduce its product range or trading
       area below 80% of the demised floor area;
   (d) The concession shall terminate immediately if FashionZone enters
       administration, liquidation, CVA or any insolvency process.

4. POST-CONCESSION RENT
   From 1 January 2025, the full annual rent of £364,000 shall be
   reinstated automatically without further notice.

5. NO PRECEDENT
   This concession does not set a precedent for any future rent review,
   rent reduction or claim by the Tenant. It is granted on an entirely
   without prejudice and ex gratia basis.

6. BACKGROUND CONTEXT
   The Landlord acknowledges the following adverse factors at the Centre:
   - HomeStyle Ltd (Unit 4) vacated in August 2025 [NOTE: this letter
     was written in June 2024 when HomeStyle signalled intent to leave]
   - CafeChain Ltd (Unit 5) has given notice to surrender (Mar 2025)
   - Footfall across the Centre declined 22% year-on-year (2023 vs 2024)
   - The Tenant reports like-for-like sales down 18% (H1 2024 vs H1 2023)

This letter is not a deed and does not formally vary the Lease. It records
a temporary commercial arrangement between the parties.
