LEASE REFERENCE
---------------
Lease dated 1 April 2019 in respect of Unit 4 ("the Lease")

NOTE: The original Lease document is NOT available in this data room.
HomeStyle was forfeited from Unit 4 in August 2025 due to accumulated
rent arrears. The Lease and this amendment are included only as references
found in the Landlord's solicitors' correspondence file.

BACKGROUND
----------
A. HomeStyle Ltd has been trading from Unit 4 as a home furnishings
   retailer since April 2019.
B. The Tenant has experienced financial difficulty following the cost-of-
   living downturn in 2022–2023 and has requested a temporary rent reduction.
C. The Landlord has agreed to a temporary reduction in exchange for
   the Tenant committing to the residual term.

VARIATIONS
----------
1. TEMPORARY RENT REDUCTION
   For the period 1 April 2023 to 31 March 2024 (12 months), the annual
   rent is reduced from:
   £89,600 per annum (£16.00/sq ft) → £67,200 per annum (£12.00/sq ft)
   
   Concession value: £22,400 (25% reduction)

   NOTE: From 1 April 2024, the full rent of £89,600 p.a. was reinstated.
   However, HomeStyle fell into arrears from July 2024 onwards. Total
   arrears at forfeiture (August 2025): £48,200.

2. SUBLET CONSENT
   The Landlord grants conditional consent for the Tenant to sub-let the
   rear section of Unit 4 (approximately 1,200 sq ft) to a compatible
   retail operator, subject to:
   (a) Sub-tenant being of acceptable financial standing;
   (b) Sub-let rent being not less than £14.00/sq ft (market rate);
   (c) Sub-lease term not exceeding the unexpired Lease term;
   (d) No further sub-letting without Landlord's consent.

   STATUS: HomeStyle did not ultimately sub-let any part of Unit 4.

3. COMMITMENT TO TERM
   In consideration of the rent reduction, the Tenant waived its Year 3
   break option (which would have fallen on 31 March 2022 — already passed
   at the date of this Deed, so this is a confirmatory waiver only).

4. ALL OTHER TERMS unchanged.
