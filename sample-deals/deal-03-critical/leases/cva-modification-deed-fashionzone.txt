DATED: 10 February 2026

PARTIES

(1) LANDLORD: Parkway Retail Fund Ltd (In Administration)

    CVA Supervisor: Restructure Partners LLP

PROPERTY
--------
Unit 1, Parkway Retail Centre, Manchester Road, Stockport, SK1 3UA
NIA: 14,000 sq ft

ORIGINAL LEASE
--------------
Lease dated 1 October 2016 ("the Lease")

RECITALS
--------
A. FashionZone Limited entered into a Company Voluntary Arrangement on
   10 February 2026 following a proposal to creditors dated 15 January
   2026 ("the CVA Proposal").

B. The CVA provides for modification of the Tenant's liabilities under
   the Lease for the residual term.

C. The Landlord (acting through the Joint Administrators) did not vote
   against the CVA Proposal but has reserved rights in respect of any
   shortfall in rent as an unsecured creditor.

CVA MODIFICATIONS TO LEASE
----------------------------
With effect from 1 February 2026 and continuing until the earlier of:
  (i) the expiry of the Lease (30 September 2026), or
  (ii) the termination of the CVA:

1. RENT REDUCTION
   The annual rent payable under the Lease is reduced from:
   £364,000 per annum (£26.00/sq ft) → £254,800 per annum (£18.20/sq ft)

   This represents a 30% reduction applied uniformly.
   Monthly rent: £21,233.33 (payable monthly in advance under CVA terms,
   notwithstanding quarterly payment provisions in the Lease).

2. SERVICE CHARGE REDUCTION
   Service charge reduced from £28,000 p.a. to £19,600 p.a. (30% reduction).

3. RENT ARREARS
   Pre-CVA rent arrears of £62,300 (being arrears accumulated from
   November 2025 to January 2026) are compromised and rank as unsecured
   CVA debts. The CVA proposes payment of 15p in the £ over 24 months,
   subject to the Tenant meeting ongoing CVA obligations.

   Projected recovery for Landlord: £62,300 × 15% = £9,345

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
   The rent deposit of £91,000 (held pursuant to Clause 3 of the Lease)
   has been applied in full against the arrears. Deposit balance: £nil.
   The CVA does not require a replacement deposit.

5. TRADING COVENANT
   Under the CVA, FashionZone commits to:
   (a) Keeping the store open and trading for a minimum of 5 days per week;
   (b) Maintaining the shop front and unit in a reasonable condition;
   (c) Not assigning, sub-letting or parting with possession during the
       CVA period.

6. MONITORING
   Restructure Partners LLP shall provide monthly trading reports to the
   Joint Administrators. If FashionZone fails to make 2 consecutive monthly
   rent payments under the CVA, the CVA shall terminate and the full Lease
   obligations shall be reinstated.

7. EXPIRY / RENEWAL
   No renewal intention has been signalled by FashionZone. The CVA envisions
   the Lease expiring by effluxion of time on 30 September 2026 without
   renewal. FashionZone has no security of tenure (the Lease was contracted
   out of the Landlord and Tenant Act 1954, ss.24-28).

LANDLORD'S RESERVED RIGHTS
----------------------------
The Joint Administrators note that the full rent shortfall for the CVA
period (approximately £54,600) ranks as an unsecured claim in the
administration of Parkway Retail Fund Ltd. The Joint Administrators
reserve the right to submit a proof of debt in the CVA for the shortfall.
